InterMine

Biologically specific Extensions to the InterMine Data-Warehousing Webservices

These modules provide interfaces to the biologically specific elements of the InterMine webservices. At present this consists of the ability to return results from queries in Biologically specific formats (GFF3, UCSC-BED, FASTA). The methods for accessing these formats provide mechanisms for iterating over the contents in logical chunks.
