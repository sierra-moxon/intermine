Use bags in template queries

The bags that we have created will be saved temporarily for our current session. To save them permanently in FlyMine you can create a MyMine account.

In MyMine you can save bags and queries you create in the QueryBuilder. You can even use the QueryBuilder to turn a new query into a template of your own.
