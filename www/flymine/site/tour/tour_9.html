Bag details page

The bag details is a great way to find out more about a list of genes. A number of 'widgets' display graphs and tables to summarise the contents. These are interactive - click to see lists of genes in each catergory,

Coming soon - we plan to add more widgets and functionality to the bag details page.

The GO enrichment and KEGG widgets suggest that this list of genes contains a higher that expected number of transcription factors.

Template queries have been executed on the whole bag, just like on the gene report page. This is a simple way to retrieve further information about the entire list. For example, you could retrieve all the exons or protein sequences.
