FlyMine can operate on custom lists of data, we call them bags. You can save bags from results pages or create them by uploading lists of identifiers. Bags can be used when running template queries and analysed by a series of widgets on a bag details page. You can also perform set operations on bags.

Coming soon - we will create globally available bags with, for example, lists of genes identified in a particular paper.

Click on 'bags' in the top menu bar to get to the bag upload page.

FlyMine's bag creation tool helps you upload a list of identifiers, the list can contain a mix of identifier types.
